## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone  
- Do not approve unknown "collect requests" in UPI apps  
- Always verify the sender before making payments  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages  
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible  
- Do not install unknown apps or allow screen sharing with strangers  
- Regularly update your apps and phone software  

### Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter a PIN to receive money  
- Double-check UPI IDs and payment details before sending money  

### Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
